Sample Task Examples

A few task examples taken from some of my class projects.


An example from a CPSC 344 project:

Alex travels between cities often and loves finding new restaurants to try out. Since his location changes, he has a list of restaurants within walking distance (since he doesn’t have a car) and from there chooses the restaurant he wishes to go to. Alex makes a decision by gathering opinions of others who have also visited the restaurant to ensure the candidate business is worth his time as he is a picky eater.



An example from a CPSC 444 project:

John is hosting a small social event at his house. He wants to play music that satisfies all of his guests’ music tastes. He has his own playlist, but wants his friends to be able to add songs to it as well. (John wants a tool that allows his guests to add to the playlist, so that their songs play at some point later in the night. - same as last sentence?) His friends all use different services to play music with, but are able to add music to his playlist.
